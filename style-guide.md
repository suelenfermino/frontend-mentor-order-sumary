# Front-end Style Guide

## Layout

The designs were created to the following widths:

- Mobile: 375px
- Desktop: 1440px

## Colors

### Primary

- Pale blue: hsl(225, 100%, 94%)bg card

### Neutral

- Very pale blue: hsl(225, 100%, 98%) bg
